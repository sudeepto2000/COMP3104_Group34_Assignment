George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology, located in Toronto, Ontario. It offers a wide range of programs including technology, health sciences, business, and design.
George Brown College was founded in 1967 and has three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. The college provides experiential learning opportunities and has partnerships with many industries.
George Brown College emphasizes hands-on learning and offers a range of programs in collaboration with industry professionals, ensuring that students gain relevant skills and practical experience.
